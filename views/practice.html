Just Show Up.

You don't need experience. You are enough, just as you are.

01

Arrive & Get Welcomed

Drift in over 15 minutes. Socialize, grab a warm burrito and coffee, and engage at your own pace. New folks get a quick, friendly orientation.

02

Gentle Check-in

A volunteer does a low-key attendance check. First name or nickname only. It is informal and privacy-respecting.

03

Balance & Grounding

We begin with simple balancing on boards and micro-movements. It’s okay to hold onto a friend or a tree. This activates the body before the mind.

04

Community-Led Tai Chi

As the city clock chimes, the group eases into motion. Led by peers, the flow is simple, repetitive, and focused on weight shifts and breathing. (20-30 mins).

05

Closing Circle

We close with a short learning circle where members can lead a 5-minute motivation, share announcements, and offer encouragement.

06

Checkout Support

A brief checkout where participants may receive small engagement supports (essentials) tied to their attendance.
