The Daily Flow

01

Arrive & Get Welcomed (9:30 AM)

Drift in. Grab a warm burrito and coffee. Socialize or stay quiet—engage at your own pace.

02

Gentle Check-in

A volunteer does a low-key attendance check. First name or nickname only. It’s informal and privacy-respecting.

03

Balance & Grounding

We begin with simple balancing on boards and micro-movements. It’s okay to hold onto a friend or a tree.

04

Community-Led Tai Chi

As the city clock chimes, the group eases into motion. Led by peers, the flow is simple, repetitive, and focused on weight shifts. (20-30 mins)

05

Closing Circle

We close with a short learning circle where members can lead a 5-minute motivation, share announcements, or offer encouragement.

06

Checkout Support

A brief checkout where participants may receive small engagement supports tied to attendance.
