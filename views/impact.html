Measuring Stability

We don't guess. We count. Here is how we define our key performance indicators (KPIs).

5,500+ Meals

The Definition

A "Meal" is defined as a high-protein, calorie-dense unit (typically a burrito + coffee) handed directly to a participant. We do not count "snacks" or "water only" in this metric.

200+ Sessions

The Definition

A "Session" is a facilitated, 60-minute event in a public space, led by a trained peer facilitator. Casual gatherings without a facilitator are not counted.

75% Retention

The Definition

Defined as participants who return for a second session within 14 days of their first visit. This measures our ability to create immediate safety and belonging.

Risk & Safety Protocols

We operate in public spaces with vulnerable populations. Safety is not an accident; it is a design choice.

1. The Clinical Boundary

We are not a clinical mental health provider. We do not diagnose, prescribe, or treat. We provide "Pre-Clinical Stabilization." All serious medical or psychiatric needs are referred to our qualified partners.

2. De-Escalation First

Our volunteers are trained in non-violent de-escalation. We maintain a "low-barrier" but "high-boundary" environment. Violence, predation, or weapons result in immediate removal from the circle.

3. Data Privacy

We do not collect government IDs or biometric data. We track engagement via first name/nickname only to protect the dignity and anonymity of our participants.
