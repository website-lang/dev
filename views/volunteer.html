The Morning Logistics

You are the unseen hands that make the practice possible. It starts in your kitchen and ends in the park.

1. The Prep (At Home)

You pick up the supplies from storage. Using our provided equipment, you brew the coffee and warm the frozen burritos in the comfort of your own home.

2. The Transport

You bring the warmth to the park. Simply drop off the cooler and the coffee carafe at the circle. No heavy setup required‚Äîjust delivery.

3. The Retrieval

Closing the loop. After the practice, you swing by to pick up the empty carafe and cooler, ensuring we are ready for the next day.
