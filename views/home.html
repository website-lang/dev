The Missing Link

Survival Mode is Loud.
We Lower the Volume.

For our neighbors experiencing homelessness or displacement, the nervous system is often stuck in "fight or flight." In that state, navigating complex systems like healthcare, housing applications, or employment feels impossible.

Street Tai Chi is the On-Ramp. It is a non-verbal, low-barrier practice that regulates the body first. We don't demand you "get fixed" before you join. We invite you to breathe.
