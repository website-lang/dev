Why Movement Matters

Living in survival mode keeps the nervous system on high alert. It is hard to think, plan, or rest when the body is bracing for the next crisis.

We do not just offer exercise. We use Tai Chi as a tool for regulation. It is a gentle, dignified way to lower cortisol, improve focus, and reclaim a sense of ownership over your own body.
