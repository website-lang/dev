The On-Ramp

We Are Not the Destination.

Many people experiencing trauma or homelessness aren't ready for complex systems like healthcare or workforce services. They need stabilization first.

Understanding Us provides that on-ramp through consistent, peer-led movement and community. We help people regain the agency needed to make the choice to move forward.

[Image: Community Circle in Park]

The Practice: Street Tai Chi

We use gravity to anchor the body. By focusing on weight transfer and balance, we help clear the "noise" of trauma so the nervous system can down-regulate.
